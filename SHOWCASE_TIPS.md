## 📱 Device-Specific Tips

### On Desktop/Laptop
- **Move your mouse** - Show the cursor glow effect and card tilt interactions
- **Hover over elements** - Demonstrate all the hover states
- **Scroll smoothly** - Let animations play out fully
- **Show browser dev tools** (optional) - For technical audiences, briefly show clean code

### On Tablet
- **Rotate the device** - Show how layout adapts
- **Tap elements** - Demonstrate touch interactions
- **Scroll through sections** - Show responsive breakpoints

### On Phone
- **Portrait and landscape** - Show both orientations
- **Scroll performance** - Demonstrate smooth scrolling even on mobile
- **Touch interactions** - Show how buttons and cards respond
