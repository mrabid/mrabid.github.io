📄 Connecting Patients with Healthcare Providers: A Web-Based eHealth Platform

A comprehensive research paper exploring the development and impact of a web application designed to enhance connectivity between patients and healthcare providers.

  • 📚 Type: Research Paper / Publication
  • 🏛️ Institution: Independent University, Bangladesh
  • 📖 Published In: Proceedings of the Second International Conference on Artificial Intelligence
  • 🔗 Paper Link: View Paper on Google Books

📝 Abstract

This paper explores the development and impact of a web application designed to enhance connectivity between patients and healthcare providers. The study examines key components such as user experience, system functionality, security, and the integration of telemedicine, with the ultimate goal of improving healthcare accessibility and patient engagement.

🔬 Research Overview

Through in-depth analysis, the paper highlights the transformative potential of user-centric design and the application of digital tools in healthcare. The findings underscore the importance of seamless communication, navigation, and security in driving patient satisfaction and system efficiency.

This research contributes valuable insights for future healthcare innovations by showcasing how digital platforms can reshape the patient-provider dynamic, ultimately promoting more accessible, responsive, and personalized healthcare services.

🎯 Key Research Areas

  • 👤 User Experience (UX) - Analyzing how patients interact with healthcare web applications
  • ⚙️ System Functionality - Evaluating the technical components and their effectiveness
  • 🔒 Security Protocols - Ensuring patient data protection and privacy compliance
  • 🏥 Telemedicine Integration - Examining remote healthcare delivery mechanisms
  • 📊 Patient Engagement - Measuring the impact on healthcare accessibility

💡 Key Findings

  • ✅ Seamless communication and navigation are critical for patient satisfaction
  • ✅ Security protocols play a vital role in building patient trust
  • ✅ Telemedicine integration significantly improves healthcare accessibility
  • ✅ User-centric design principles drive system efficiency and patient engagement
  • ✅ Digital platforms enable more personalized healthcare services

🌟 Research Impact

This research contributes valuable insights for future healthcare innovations by demonstrating how digital platforms can:

  • 🔄 Reshape the patient-provider dynamic
  • 🌐 Promote more accessible healthcare services
  • ⚡ Enable responsive and real-time medical consultations
  • 🎯 Deliver personalized healthcare experiences
